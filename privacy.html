Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Welcome to SHIFT. Your privacy matters to us, and we want to be transparent about how we handle your information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

Your email address and display name, provided when you create an account via email/password, Google Sign-In, or Sign in with Apple. A unique user ID is assigned by our authentication provider to associate your activity with your account.

Session and Usage Data

Information about how you interact with the app, including:

  • Sessions you start, pause, resume, or complete — including session name, category, and duration
  • Time of day and day of week when sessions are used
  • How far you scroll on the home screen (tracked at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% intervals)
  • Buttons and features you interact with
  • Total time spent in the app per session
  • Whether you have completed your first session (recorded once per account)
  • Which session information pop-ups you open during a guided session

Device and Technical Information

Non-personal technical details including device type, operating system version, and app version. Crash reports and performance diagnostics may also be collected to help us identify and fix issues.

We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, media files, microphone audio, or camera data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account and the features of the app
  • To save your favourites and preferences
  • To understand how the app is used so we can improve the experience
  • To identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • To communicate important updates about the app or your account

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or marketers. We only share data with the third-party service providers listed above, and only as necessary to operate the app.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account remains active. If you request deletion of your account or data, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days of that request. Anonymised, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication, and access controls on our database. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your account information from within the app's Profile section
  • Request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us directly
  • Manage app permissions through your device's settings

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable laws such as the GDPR (European Union) or CCPA (California). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tsakiriszaylin@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

SHIFT is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
